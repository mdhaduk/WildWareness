Sonoma Veterans Building

West Sonoma, Sonoma County

Name: Sonoma Veterans Building

Address: 126 1st Street

City: West Sonoma

Contact: 707-938-4105

Status: Open

The Sonoma Veterans Memorial Building, located near Sonoma's historic downtown, is available for event rentals. It offers various amenities, including corporate meeting services, outdoor spaces, and areas for ceremonies and receptions. The facility includes an auditorium with a capacity of 700 (500 seated), a dining room for up to 309 people, and a dance area. Rental rates range from $55 to $75 per hour, with additional charges for late hours. Discounts are available for non-peak days, multi-day rentals, and non-profit organizations. To reserve, contact the building via phone or email during business hours.
