Contra Costa College (CCC) is a vibrant public community college located in West Contra Costa County, California. It serves a diverse and growing student population, offering a wide range of educational opportunities that cater to the needs of its local and regional community. The college is dedicated to providing an accessible, affordable, and high-quality education with a focus on inclusivity, academic excellence, and student success.

The college offers a variety of programs, including associate degrees, certificates, and vocational training, with pathways to transfer to four-year universities. These programs are designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in the workforce, continue their academic pursuits, and contribute meaningfully to society. Additionally, Contra Costa College supports lifelong learning through its community outreach initiatives and continuing education programs, aiming to serve students at all stages of their academic and professional journeys.

Situated on 83 acres of scenic land, CCC features modern facilities, including state-of-the-art classrooms, laboratories, and recreational areas. The college has a rich history, built on a legacy of innovation and community engagement, and continues to develop new programs and partnerships to meet the evolving needs of its students. The college is committed to fostering a culture of growth and personal development, ensuring that students are equipped to face both local and global challenges.

With an emphasis on equity, CCC works to create an inclusive environment that supports students from all backgrounds, particularly those who are traditionally underserved in higher education. The college's faculty and staff are dedicated to providing individualized support, mentorship, and resources to help students succeed academically and professionally. By focusing on equity and access, Contra Costa College strives to empower students and improve the socio-economic mobility of its community.

At its core, Contra Costa College's mission is to provide effective learning experiences that foster academic success, personal growth, and community engagement. The college plays a pivotal role in the social and economic development of the surrounding areas, preparing students for success in their careers, further education, and beyond. Through its commitment to inclusivity, innovation, and excellence, CCC continues to be a beacon of opportunity for students seeking to make a positive impact in their communities.
