Oak Fire
Santa Barbara, Sisquoc
Description:

The Oak Fire was a fast-moving wildfire that erupted in Tepusquet Canyon near Santa Maria, California, on January 1, 2025.
gniting in dry, rugged terrain, the fire quickly spread across 42 acres, fueled by strong winds and parched vegetation.
Evacuation orders were swiftly issued for nearby residents as firefighters from multiple agencies mobilized to combat the
flames. Aerial water drops and ground crews worked relentlessly to establish containment lines, navigating challenging
topography to prevent the fire from spreading to homes and agricultural lands. Despite the difficult conditions, emergency
responders managed to contain the fire before it caused significant structural damage or casualties.
