Status: Open

The Sebastopol Community Cultural Center (SCCC) serves as a vibrant space celebrating community culture and well-being. Located in Sebastopol, California, SCCC offers a variety of events, including live music performances, classes, and workshops. It supports local arts and hosts both large and small performances, featuring artists from diverse genres. The center provides opportunities for youth programs and facility rentals. Additionally, the Folk Farm Music School, a part of SCCC, offers lessons in acoustic roots music. SCCC is dedicated to enriching the local community through creativity and connection, with events like The Wildwoods and Joe Craven performances scheduled for 2025.

Their mission is fostering community culture, enrichment, and well-being. Founded in 1982, it serves Sebastopol and surrounding areas, providing educational, recreational, and service programs for all ages. As a nonprofit organization, it supports itself through service fees, fundraising, and donations. SCCC's mission is to bring people together through various cultural and educational events, while ensuring equal access to services for everyone, regardless of age, gender, race, or national origin. Donations are vital for the center's operations and community impact.

The Center also provides oportunities to make donations and volunteer. Please visit their website for more information.
